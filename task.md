The 3-Reels-a-Week System

Every week, three reels go up from your college's presence — no exceptions, no "nothing happened this week." The formula below repeats every week so your Community Lead can execute it without waiting on you.

100% HUMAN-SHOT. NO AI-GENERATED CONTENT AT THE CAMPUS LEVEL.

These reels are filmed by you, your Domain Leads, and Foot Soldiers — on your own phones, with real students on camera. That authenticity is the entire point: it's what makes a reel feel like it came from campus, not from a brand account, and it's what earns the organic share that gets it collab-worthy. Any AI-generated reel library the main Clstr account runs is a separate, central system — it is not a substitute for your own content.

The weekly formula

•  Reel 1 — MEME. Trending audio or format, made campus-specific. This is your reach engine — highest share rate, lowest production effort.

•  Reel 2 — CAMPUS CULTURE or STUDENT STORY. Real students, real moments (canteen, hostel, events, wins). This builds "is this really my campus?" trust.

•  Reel 3 — STUDENT CONVERSATION or BRANDING. Vox-pops, testimonials, or a direct feature moment (a club president posting live, a milestone recap).

Who shoots it

•  Community Lead owns the weekly reel calendar and is the default shooter/editor.

•  Foot Soldiers help film on the day — extra hands for crowd shots, multiple angles, candid reactions.

•  You (Campus Captain) appear on camera for branding/milestone reels — face-to-camera builds trust faster than a voiceover.

•  Students featured in culture/story reels are filmed spontaneously with their consent — no scripts, no retakes for polish. Rough and real beats polished and generic.

•  Editing stays light — trim, trending audio, text overlay, end card. A phone editing app (CapCut/InShot) is enough. Do not over-produce; an overly polished reel reads as an ad and gets scrolled past.

Non-negotiable rules for every reel

•  Tag @clstr.in and request the Instagram Collab feature on every post — this is what pushes your college's content onto the main Clstr account's reach, not just your own followers.

•  Use #ClstrOn[YourCollege] on every reel for trackability.

•  Brand constants: Space Grotesk Bold text overlays, lime #CCFF00 on black #000000 for any text card, end card with the Clstr end-screen every time.

•  Would students actually send this to their college WhatsApp group? If the answer is no, don't post it — this is the only filter that matters.

•  Raw footage goes to the Clstr content team the same day it's shot, even if you've already posted — the main account may reuse it.
